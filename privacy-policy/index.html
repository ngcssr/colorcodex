Privacy Policy

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Color Codex handles information when you use this website. By using the site, you agree to the practices described here.

Information you provide

Most tools work directly in your browser. If you type a color value, upload an image, or interact with a picker, that information is used to provide the tool result. Uploaded images are processed locally in the browser for color picking features and are not intentionally stored by this site.

Local storage

The site may use browser storage such as localStorage to remember preferences, language selection, color history, and other interface settings. You can clear this data in your browser settings at any time.

Analytics and logs

Basic technical data may be processed by hosting, analytics, security, or performance services, such as page URL, device type, browser type, approximate region, referring page, and error information. This data helps keep the site secure, fast, and reliable.

Cookies and third-party services

If analytics, ads, embedded services, or security services are used, those providers may set cookies or process data according to their own policies. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be processed by service providers that help operate, secure, analyze, or improve the site, or when required by law.

Children

This site is intended for general audiences and is not directed to children under the age required by applicable privacy law.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as the site changes. The updated version will be posted on this page.
